Rule Category Implementation Logic Impact & Priority
Foundation Market Events Scans Ticketmaster/Eventbrite signals. Applies +15% to +50% premiums based on impact score. Highest priority pricing signal. Forms the "Tentpole" for revenue surge during festivals/holidays.
Strategic Seasonal Profiles Date-range based adjustments (e.g., Summer/Winter). Percentage adjustment to base rate. Predictable base shift. Aligned with historic neighborhood demand curves.
Strategic Last-Minute Ramp Gradual Curve tapering from e.g., 30% discount at Day 1 to 5% at Day 15. Protects occupancy by filling inventory as check-in approaches. Curbs revenue loss on orphan nights.
Inventory Occupancy Trigger Adjusts price based on property-level pacing. Tries to hit 85% target before shifting to premium rates. Aggressive pacing logic. Surges rates when "The Machine" detects high booking velocity.
Inventory Gap Filling Identifies 1-3 night gaps between bookings. Automates 10-20% discounts specifically for those dates. Yield optimizer. Recovers revenue that would typically be lost due to strict min-stay constraints.
Integrity Price Guardrails Absolute Floor & Ceiling prices. Applied at the final pass. Safety Net. No matter how high the surge or deep the discount, engine never violates these bounds.

🧠 Alignment Note

Current codebase logic strictly follows the Waterfall Principle: Rules in Pass 4 (Integrity) overwrite results from all previous passes. For example, if a 40% Last-Minute discount would push the price to 250 AED, but the Floor Price is set to 300 AED, the final proposal will be 300 AED.
